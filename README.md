<h2 align="center">📂 Deep-Dive Featured Systems & Architectures</h2>

#### 📡 [Adika AI Interviewer](https://github.com/Adi3182004/Adika-Ai-Interviewer) — Full-Stack AI Career Intelligence
> **Commercial-Grade Adaptive Assessment & Schedule Generation Engine**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | Traditional hiring platforms parse resumes statically without evaluating candidate problem-solving depth in real-time. |
> | **Solution** | Full-stack platform built with TanStack Start (SSR) and React 19. Runs an adaptive AI grading engine using Vercel AI SDK, SpaCy, Scikit-Learn, and NetworkX skill graphs. |
> | **Stack** | React 19, TanStack Start, Tailwind v4, FastAPI, Python, SQLAlchemy, Supabase PostgreSQL, NetworkX, Bun. |

<br>

#### 🕵️ [EvidenceOS](https://github.com/Adi3182004/Evidence-OS) — Digital Forensics & Incident Platform
> **Enterprise Collaborative Intelligence & Investigation Timeline Reconstruction**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | Security analysts struggle to parse scattered EXIF headers, syslog timestamps, and chat exports without breaking evidence chain-of-custody. |
> | **Solution** | A Palantir-modeled dashboard linking suspect activity timelines in interactive D3.js knowledge graphs backed by cryptographic SHA-256 integrity logs. |
> | **Stack** | React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Python (FastAPI/Uvicorn), PostgreSQL/Supabase, D3.js. |

<br>

#### 📨 [SilentNet](https://github.com/Adi3182004/SilentNet) — Offline Mobile Mesh Communication
> **Decentralized Local-First P2P Messenger & Emergency Telemetry System**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | Centralized mobile towers fail during natural disasters, remote expeditions, or infrastructure blackouts. |
> | **Solution** | Peer-to-peer mesh routing over Bluetooth Low Energy (Nearby Connections API). Runs embedded Python inside Android via Chaquopy, encrypted SQLCipher Room database, and background sync via WorkManager. |
> | **Stack** | Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Chaquopy (Python inside Android), Room ORM, SQLCipher, WorkManager, Nearby P2P API. |

<br>

#### 🎹 [Key-Strokebased-Auth](https://github.com/Adi3182004/Key-Strokebased-Auth) — Continuous Biometric Dynamics
> **Real-Time Typing Telemetry & Session Hijack Prevention Engine**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | One-time 2FA login cannot protect active user sessions if the device is hijacked post-authentication. |
> | **Solution** | Continuous biometric validation measuring keystroke fly times and dwell times. Trains Scikit-Learn Random Forest models to lock sessions upon anomalous typing velocity. |
> | **Stack** | Python, Flask, Scikit-learn, Joblib, Keystroke Dynamics, Twilio Telemetry API. |

<br>

#### 👤 [TrueFace](https://github.com/Adi3182004/TrueFace) — AI Media Verification & Deepfake Detector
> **Generative AI Detection & Media Authenticity Quiz System**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | Generative AI deepfakes pollute online information ecosystems, bypassing standard media analysis. |
> | **Solution** | Performs deep visual header and CLAHE contrast inspections using OpenCV pipelines, integrated with Google Gemini API fact verification. |
> | **Stack** | Python, Flask, OpenCV, Face-Recognition Library, SQLite, Google Gemini API. |

<br>

#### 🤖 [ARDA](https://github.com/Adi3182004/Autonomous-Risk-Decision-Agent-ARDA-) — Autonomous Risk Decision Agent
> **Structured State-Machine Risk Assessment Engine**
> 
> | Attribute | Implementation & Architecture Details |
> | :--- | :--- |
> | **Problem** | High-velocity risk ingestion systems need automated, deterministic score verification without human latency. |
> | **Solution** | Engineered asynchronous worker queues and rule engines to ingest multi-variable risk vectors and output machine-readable risk scores. |
> | **Stack** | Python, Automated Agents, Async Process Workers, Rule Engine State Machines. |
